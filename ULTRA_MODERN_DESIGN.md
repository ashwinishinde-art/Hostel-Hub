## 🎬 Animation Details

### **Hover Animations**
- Cards lift: 15-20px translateY
- Scale: up to 1.05x
- Shadows upgrade: standard → XL
- Border colors: smooth transition
- Icons: rotate and scale
- Durations: 0.4s cubic-bezier

### **Load Animations**
- Fade in from bottom: 40px translate
- Staggered timing: 0.1s between items
- Duration: 0.8s
- Easing: cubic-bezier(0.175, 0.885, 0.32, 1.275)

### **Background Animations**
- Gradient shift: 15s infinite loop
- Floating circles: 8-10s animation
- SVG patterns: continuous slide
- Multiple depth layers
